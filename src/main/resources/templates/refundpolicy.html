Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

At SwapTicket, we aim to ensure a smooth and secure ticket exchange process. This Refund Policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may or may not be issued.

1 No Refund After Purchase

Once a ticket or passenger seat is purchased through SwapTicket, the transaction is considered final.

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Incorrect selection of ticket/passenger
  • Travel disruptions (train delays, cancellations, etc.)—please refer to the railway authority for such issues

2 Invalid or Fraudulent Tickets

If a purchased ticket is found to be invalid, cancelled, or fake:

  • Buyers must report the issue within 24 hours of purchase
  • SwapTicket will investigate the complaint, and if the claim is verified, the buyer may receive a full refund
⚠️ Warning: Sellers found submitting fraudulent tickets will be permanently banned and may face legal action.

3 Refund Processing

  • Refunds (if approved) will be processed to the original payment method or user wallet within 5–7 business days
  • SwapTicket reserves the right to deduct processing or administrative fees, if applicable

4 Journey Not Completed or Ticket Unused

Refunds will not be issued if:

  • The buyer fails to board the train
  • The journey is not completed due to personal reasons
  • The seller fails to provide post-sale support (e.g., passenger info) unless proven fraudulent

5 SwapTicket's Rights

  • SwapTicket acts as a neutral facilitator and is not obligated to mediate disputes unless a clear violation of terms is evident
  • We reserve the right to amend or cancel refund requests that do not meet the conditions stated above

Refund Policy

Effective Date: June 17, 2025

At SwapTicket, we act solely as a digital platform that connects users who wish to list and share details of their confirmed train bookings with interested travelers. We do not issue, resell, or guarantee tickets. This Refund Policy explains when refunds may apply for payments made through our platform.

1 No Refund After Confirmation

Once a user completes a transaction (for example, purchasing a listed passenger slot), it is treated as final.

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Change of mind or plan after purchase
  • Incorrect selection of train, passenger, or journey details
  • Travel cancellations or delays by railway authorities (refer to IRCTC for official refunds)

2 Invalid or Misrepresented Listings

If a buyer reports that the ticket information provided by a seller is invalid or fraudulent:

  • The issue must be reported within 24 hours of the transaction
  • SwapTicket will investigate the complaint, and if verified, a full refund may be initiated
⚠️ Important: Any seller found listing fraudulent or altered ticket details will face permanent account suspension and possible legal action.

3 Refund Processing Time

  • Approved refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • SwapTicket may deduct administrative or transaction fees where applicable.

4 Platform’s Role and Limitations

  • SwapTicket serves only as a neutral facilitator connecting users. We do not own, issue, or control train tickets.
  • We are not responsible for disputes between users unless a violation of our platform policy is proven.
  • Refund eligibility will be decided solely based on transaction verification and supporting evidence.

5 Policy Changes

  • SwapTicket reserves the right to modify or update this Refund Policy without prior notice.
  • Any updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 16 May 2025

At SwapTicket, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our platform to list or explore journey details shared by other users.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to operate and improve our services:

Personal Information

Your name, email address, and optionally your contact number for account registration and communication.

Journey Details

Information such as train number, journey date, route (from/to), and PNR number for listing or browsing travel details.

Uploaded Files

Users may upload ticket images or ID proofs solely for verification. These are securely stored and visible only under authorized access conditions (e.g., after a confirmed match between users).

Transaction Details

Payment identifiers, transaction IDs, and status information are handled securely through our third-party payment partner (Razorpay). We do not store card or bank information on our servers.

Session Information

We use session-based cookies to maintain your login status and enhance user experience. No sensitive personal data is stored in cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Facilitate safe communication and interaction between users on the platform.
  • Display relevant journey listings and manage your dashboard.
  • Enable secure payment processing through authorized partners.
  • Prevent misuse, detect fraudulent activities, and ensure platform integrity.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade user data. We only share limited data when necessary to operate our services, including:

  • With payment processors (e.g., Razorpay) to handle transactions securely.
  • With other users, only as required for completing a valid journey listing interaction.
  • With regulatory authorities or law enforcement when legally required.

4. Data Retention

  • Journey data is retained until two days after the journey date, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • Personal account data is retained as long as your account remains active or as required by law.

5. Security

We apply strong security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Database encryption and secured access control.
  • Session and CSRF protection for user accounts.
  • Periodic audits to ensure compliance with data security standards.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for specific data uses.
  • Contact us regarding any privacy issue at swap2ticket@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates Razorpay for payment processing and Cloudinary for secure file hosting. Each third-party service has its own privacy policies, which you can review on their respective websites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.

By using SwapTicket, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.

Note: SwapTicket is an independent community platform and is not affiliated with IRCTC or Indian Railways.

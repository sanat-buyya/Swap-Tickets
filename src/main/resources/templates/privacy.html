Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [16-05-2025]

At SwapTicket, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform to buy or sell train tickets.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

Name, email address, phone number (optional).

Ticket Details

PNR number, train number, journey details, and passenger information (e.g., age, gender).

Uploaded Files

Ticket images and identity documents (only shown to buyer after purchase).

Transaction Details

Payment IDs, amount, and transaction history.

Session Information

Your login email to maintain session state securely.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Facilitate secure ticket exchanges between buyers and sellers.
  • Process payments and manage wallets (including admin, seller).
  • Show your tickets and transactions in your dashboard.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure authenticity of uploaded tickets.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share:

  • Ticket and passenger details with the buyer after purchase.
  • Transactional data with payment processors (e.g., Razorpay) for processing payments.
  • Information with law enforcement if required by legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

  • Ticket data is deleted automatically two days after the journey date.
  • Personal information is retained as long as your account is active or as needed for legal compliance.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information using modern security practices like:

  • Encrypted database fields where necessary.
  • Session management to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Limited access to sensitive data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account or data.
  • Contact us regarding any privacy concern at: support@swapticket.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

By using SwapTicket, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

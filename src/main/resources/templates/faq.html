Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about buying and selling train tickets on SwapTicket

What is SwapTicket?

SwapTicket is a peer-to-peer platform that allows users to safely sell or buy IRCTC train tickets. We aim to reduce scams by providing verified listings and secure payments.

How do I sell my train ticket?
  • Log in or register
  • Go to Sell Ticket
  • Fill in ticket and passenger details
  • Upload ticket image or document
  • Set your price and submit
How do I buy a ticket?
  • Visit the Buy Tickets page
  • Use filters to find suitable tickets
  • Click Buy, make the payment via Razorpay
  • After payment, full ticket details (passenger info & documents) are shown
Is payment secure?

Yes. Payments are handled through Razorpay, a secure and trusted payment gateway. Your card or UPI details are never stored by SwapTicket.

What is the service/booking fee?

We charge a small booking fee (e.g., ₹20) on top of the ticket price. This fee helps run and maintain the platform and is shown clearly before payment.

When does the seller receive the money?

Once a buyer successfully completes the payment, the ticket price is credited to the seller's wallet instantly. Sellers can withdraw funds at any time (if withdrawal is implemented).

What happens after the journey date?

Tickets are automatically removed from the platform 2 days after the journey date to keep the listings fresh and relevant.

Can I see who bought my ticket?

Yes. Go to My Tickets in your dashboard to view all your sold tickets and the buyer's email.

What if I face any issue?

You can contact our support team via the Contact Us section or reach us at support@swapticket.com

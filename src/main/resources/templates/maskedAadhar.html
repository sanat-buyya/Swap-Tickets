How to Download Masked Aadhaar

A step-by-step guide to securely download your masked Aadhaar card from the official UIDAI website

1

Visit the UIDAI Website

Go to the official UIDAI website: myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in

2

Find the Download Option

Scroll below the homepage and click on the "Download Aadhaar" option under the "My Aadhaar" section.

3

Select Aadhaar Card Option

A new window opens where you need to choose "Aadhaar Number" from the three options (Aadhaar Number, Enrolment ID Number, and Virtual ID Number).

4

Enter Details and Request OTP

Enter your Aadhaar number and captcha details, then click on "Send OTP".

5

Choose Masked Aadhaar Option

When asked if you want a masked Aadhaar, select "Yes" to get the masked version.

6

Verify with OTP

Enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number and click on "Verify and Download".

7

Download Your Masked Aadhaar

Your Masked Aadhaar will be downloaded automatically.

8

Confirmation and File Location

A confirmation message will appear indicating your Masked Aadhaar is ready for download.

Note: The file will download to your device's default download location. You can choose to save it elsewhere if needed.

9

Accessing Your Masked Aadhaar

Locate the downloaded PDF file on your device. When opened, you'll be prompted for a password.

Password Format: First 4 letters of your name (CAPITAL) + Year of birth (YYYY)
Example: For "Mahesh Kumar" born in 1990, the password would be MAHE1990

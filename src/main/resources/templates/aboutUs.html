About SwapTicket

Your trusted platform for secure train ticket exchanges

SwapTicket is a peer-to-peer platform designed to help passengers buy and sell unused train tickets safely and transparently. Inspired by the need to reduce ticket wastage and scams in WhatsApp groups, SwapTicket ensures that travelers can find genuine tickets while helping others recover their ticket costs.

Our Mission

To create a trusted, user-friendly marketplace for railway passengers to exchange tickets securely and responsibly.

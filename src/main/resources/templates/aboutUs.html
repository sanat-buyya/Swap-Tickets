About SwapTicket

Your trusted platform for secure train ticket exchanges

SwapTicket is a peer-to-peer platform that allows verified users to exchange their pre-booked train tickets safely and legally. Our goal is to reduce ticket wastage and provide a transparent, scam-free environment where travelers can find or transfer genuine tickets easily.

Our Mission

To empower train travelers with a reliable and secure digital solution for exchanging tickets responsibly and transparently.

How SwapTicket Works

We act as a facilitator between genuine sellers and interested buyers. Sellers list their valid, unused train tickets, and buyers can choose from available listings. Payments are processed through a secure payment gateway (Razorpay) and held temporarily until ticket verification is complete. Once verified, the seller receives payment directly to their registered wallet.

What We Offer

Sell Unused Tickets

List your confirmed IRCTC tickets for exchange at fair value with verified buyers.

Buy Genuine Tickets

Find valid train tickets from other users — safely and instantly.

Secure Payments

Transactions are processed via Razorpay with bank-level encryption and escrow protection.

24/7 Assistance

Our support team is available around the clock to assist with exchanges and payments.

Legal Disclaimer

SwapTicket is not affiliated with IRCTC, Indian Railways, or any government organization. We do not issue, modify, or cancel train tickets. All listings on this platform are user-generated. We only facilitate communication and secure transactions between verified users for legitimate ticket exchanges.

Users are responsible for ensuring that their listed tickets comply with IRCTC and railway policies. Any misuse, fake ticket uploads, or inflated resale pricing will result in permanent account suspension.

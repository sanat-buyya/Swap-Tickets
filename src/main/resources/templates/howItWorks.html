How It Works

Simple, secure train ticket exchanges on SwapTicket

SwapTicket is designed to make the process of selling and buying train tickets simple, secure, and scam-free. Here's how it works:
For Sellers – Sell Your Unused Ticket
1

Register & Access

  • Register/Login to your account
  • Go to Sell Ticket section
2

Enter Ticket Details

  • Train details (From, To, Date)
  • Train Number and Class
  • PNR number
3

Passenger Information

  • Passenger names, ages, genders
  • Seat numbers
  • Upload ticket proof/documents
4

Pricing & Submission

  • Set your desired price
  • Submit the ticket
  • It will be listed for buyers
5

Receive Payment

  • When sold, amount is transferred to your wallet
  • (Excluding small service fee)
For Buyers – Buy a Ticket
1

Browse Tickets

  • Visit the Buy Tickets page
  • Use filters to find tickets
  • (From, To, Date, Train Number)
2

Select & Purchase

  • Click Buy next to available seat
  • Pay securely via Razorpay
3

Access Ticket Details

  • After payment, full details are revealed
  • Passenger names, documents, seat
4

Board the Train

  • Use the purchased ticket
  • Present documents if required
Secure Payment Flow

Seller lists ticket

Buyer pays (price + fee) via Razorpay

Admin wallet receives total amount

Seller gets price, SwapTicket keeps fee

Admin Dashboard
1

Platform Overview

  • View total users
  • See active tickets
  • Monitor total revenue
2

Transaction Management

  • Track all transactions
  • Manage platform fees
  • Monitor wallet balances
3

Ticket Validity

  • Tickets auto-remove 2 days after journey
  • Only valid tickets shown to buyers
  • Ensure listings stay current
